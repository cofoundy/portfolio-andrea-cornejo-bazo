About Me

I'm a bilingual (English–Spanish) Project and Data Manager with over a decade of experience in NIH-funded public health research, international program coordination, and academic instruction. My career spans work with Yale University, the University of Central Florida, and Peru's top institutions, focusing on health equity among vulnerable populations. I hold a Master's in Political Sciences from Pompeu Fabra University (Barcelona) and a Law degree with distinction from PUCP. I combine rigorous data management with cross-cultural collaboration to advance meaningful research.
